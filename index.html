Welcome to Mwecau Living Lab

A collaborative project between Mwenge Catholic University (MWECAU), Tanzania, and Catholic University Leuven (KU Leuven), Belgium, focused on conservation of endangered plant species affected by climate change.

Our Focus Areas

Conservation

Protecting and preserving endangered plant species in the Western Usambara Mountains, Tanzania.

Community

Linking conservation efforts with community livelihoods for sustainable development.

Research

Conducting studies on climate resilience and biodiversity preservation strategies.
